Our Process

Adjust cabinet hinges for even gaps and flush closing

Check and adjust all drawer glides for proper/smooth operation

Check and tighten all cabinet component fasteners

Check and tighten cabinet knobs, pulls, handles

Check bumpers and add as needed

Inspect adjustable shelf pins and fit as needed

Inspect millwork trim and fillers for solid attachment

Inspect pull-out trash, pantry, spice rack, etc. units for proper functioning

*Concluding our work servicing your cabinets, we will submit a brief report indicating any failed components or elements that we would recommend further services or replacing, if any.
